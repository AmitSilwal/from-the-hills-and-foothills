1864 Duar War Commanders

Commanders in the 1864 Duar War

This note lists the major military and political figures involved in the 1864_Duar_War.

British Commanders

Brigadier-General Dunsford

Commanded the British column advancing from Jalpaiguri during the early phase of the campaign.

His forces captured frontier positions such as Maynaguri and Dhamonee.

Colonel Watson

Commanded the column advancing from Cooch_Behar.

His forces captured important frontier passes including:

  • Buxa
  • Balia

Brigadier-General Mulcaster

Senior British commander responsible for coordinating operations on the Assam frontier.

Colonel Richardson

Commanded operations in the eastern sector near Dewangiri.

Bhutanese Leadership

Tongso Penlop

A major Bhutanese regional governor who organized resistance against the British advance.

Bhutanese forces under his influence were involved in the counterattacks around Dewangiri.
