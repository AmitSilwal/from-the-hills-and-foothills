Dalimkote

Dalimkote was a Bhutanese hill fort located in the foothills of the eastern Dooars.

The fort controlled routes leading from the plains into Bhutan.

Historical Mentions

1864 Duar War

British forces advancing through the Dooars captured [[Dalimkote]] during the campaign described in [[1864_Duar_War]].

The capture of Dalimkote opened access to further frontier passes.
