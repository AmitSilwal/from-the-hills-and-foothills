Sources & Credits Log

This log maintains a transparent record of all sources used in the archive.

Purpose

To ensure ethical curation, proper credit, and long-term reliability.

Entry Format

  • Title
  • Content type (image / video / text / mixed)
  • Creator / Handle
  • Platform / Publication
  • Original link
  • Region
  • Cultural context
  • Notes

Example

Title: Weekly market at Kalimpong
Content type: Image
Creator: @example_handle
Platform: Instagram
Region: Darjeeling hills
Notes: Public post, credited

Maintaining this log protects both the archive and original creators.
