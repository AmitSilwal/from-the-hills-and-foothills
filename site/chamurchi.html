Chamurchi

Chamurchi is a frontier pass located in the eastern Dooars near the Bhutan border.

The pass served as an important route connecting the plains of Bengal with the Bhutan hills.

Historical Mentions

1864 Duar War

During the 1864 Duar War British forces secured the pass at Chamurchi as part of their campaign to control the Dooars frontier.

The capture of Chamurchi helped establish British control over key access points into Bhutan.
