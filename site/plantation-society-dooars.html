Plantation Society in the Dooars

After the British annexation of the Dooars in 1865, large areas of forest were cleared for tea plantations.

This led to the development of plantation settlements and labour communities.

Migration

Workers were brought from various regions including:

  • Nepal
  • Chota Nagpur
  • Central India

These migrants formed the labour force of the tea plantations.

Settlement Pattern

Plantation settlements developed in the form of:

  • tea estates
  • labour lines
  • plantation villages.
