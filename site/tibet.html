Tibet

Tibet is a high-altitude plateau region located north of the Himalayan range.

Historically Tibet maintained trade and cultural connections with Bhutan and the Himalayan frontier regions.

Historical Context

Trade routes between Tibet, Bhutan, and the plains passed through several Himalayan passes connected to the Dooars frontier.
