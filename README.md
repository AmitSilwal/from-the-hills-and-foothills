# From the Hills and Foothills

An archive of Nepali (Gorkhali) culture, life, and landscapes  
from the Dooars, Darjeeling hills, and Sikkim.


---

## About this archive
From the Hills and Foothills is a digital archive documenting everyday life,
places, language, traditions, and cultural memory of Nepali (Gorkhali)
communities across the eastern Himalayan region.

This is not a personal brand or influencer project.
It is a long-term effort focused on preservation and respectful curation.

---

## Scope
The archive includes:
- Dooars
- Darjeeling hills
- Sikkim

Content focuses on:
- Places and landscapes
- Everyday life and work
- Language and cultural memory
- Public cultural practices
- Archival notes and references

---

## Curation principles
- No face or voice of the curator
- Content sourced from public material or shared with clear permission
- All content is credited
- Respect over reach
- Documentation over opinion

---

## Credits & responsibility
All content belongs to its original creators and sources.
Credits are provided wherever applicable.

If any content requires correction or removal,
it will be addressed promptly and respectfully.

---

## Status
This archive is a living work and will grow slowly over time.
