# Archive Rules

These rules define the ethical and curatorial boundaries of
From the Hills and Foothills.

---

## Purpose
This archive exists to document and preserve Nepali (Gorkhali)
culture from the Dooars, Darjeeling hills, and Sikkim in a
respectful, non-exploitative manner.

---

## Content allowed
- Public places and landscapes
- Everyday life and work (markets, tea gardens, streets)
- Public cultural events and rituals
- Language, sayings, and cultural notes
- Historical material from public sources

---

## Content not allowed
- Private individuals without clear public context
- Sensitive or personal moments
- Political messaging
- Sensational or misleading content
- Copyrighted material without proper context or credit

---

## Credit & sourcing
Every item must include:
- Original creator or source
- Platform or publication name
- Link to original content (if available)

If the source is unclear, the content should not be published.

---

## Tone & approach
- Neutral
- Respectful
- Observational
- Archival

The archive avoids judgement, romanticization, or stereotyping.

---

## Corrections & removals
Requests for correction or removal will be handled promptly
and respectfully.
