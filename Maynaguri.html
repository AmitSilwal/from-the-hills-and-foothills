Maynaguri

Maynaguri is a settlement in the Dooars region of northern Bengal.

Historically it served as a frontier location along the Bhutan borderlands.

Historical Mentions

1864 Duar War

British forces advancing from [[Jalpaiguri]] occupied [[Maynaguri]] during the early phase of the [[1864_Duar_War]].

The position was abandoned by Bhutanese forces and secured by British troops.
