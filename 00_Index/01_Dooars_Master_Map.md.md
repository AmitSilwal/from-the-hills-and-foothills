# Observational Note

The Dooars region can be understood through three overlapping layers:

1. **Frontier Geography** – Himalayan passes linking plains and hills  
2. **Colonial Economic Transformation** – tea plantations and labour migration  
3. **Post-colonial Administrative Region**

Each note in the archive connects to one or more of these layers.
