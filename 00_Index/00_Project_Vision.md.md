# From the Hills and Foothills — Internal Vision

## Why This Exists

This archive exists to systematically document the historical, economic, and cultural transformation of the Dooars and adjoining hills–foothill region.

The objective is long-term preservation, not short-term visibility.

---

## Core Orientation

This is:
- A research-driven archive
- A structured documentation effort
- A slow institutional build

This is NOT:
- A social media growth project
- A motivational platform
- A political commentary space
- A reactive content channel

---

## Primary Focus (Initial 2–3 Years)

Geography:
- Western Dooars / specific districts (refine progressively)

Time Focus:
- Post-1865 British annexation
- Plantation expansion period
- Labour migration systems

Core Themes:
- Tea plantation structure
- Migration and settlement
- Land & forest transformation
- Demographic shifts
- Cultural adaptation

## Operating Principles

1. Facts and interpretation must be separated.
2. Every note must include:
   - Context
   - Key Points
   - Why It Matters
   - Questions Raised
3. No unverified claims.
4. Depth over speed.
5. Research before publication.
