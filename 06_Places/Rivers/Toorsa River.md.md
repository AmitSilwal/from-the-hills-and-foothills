# Torsa River

## 🟢 Quick Facts
- **Type:** Himalayan river  
- **Region:** Central Dooars  
- **Flow Direction:** North (Bhutan hills) → South (plains)  
- **Role in Dooars:** Central river influencing settlement and land division  

---

## Description
A major river flowing through the central Dooars.  
It originates in the Bhutan hills and flows southward into the plains, shaping the landscape and human geography of the region.

---

## Course & Flow
1. Originates in the Bhutan hills  
2. Enters the Dooars region  
3. Flows through central Dooars  
4. Continues into the plains  

---

## Importance
- Divides different parts of the region  
- Influences settlement patterns  
- Supports agriculture and local livelihoods  
- Contributes to seasonal flooding  

---

## Physical Features
- Seasonal variation in water flow  
- Carries silt from hills to plains  
- Alters course in flood conditions
