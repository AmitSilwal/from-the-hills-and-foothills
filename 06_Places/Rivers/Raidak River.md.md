# Raidak River

## 🟢 Quick Facts
- **Type:** Himalayan river  
- **Region:** Dooars  
- **Flow Direction:** North (Bhutan hills) → South (plains)  
- **Role in Dooars:** Part of the dynamic river system  

---

## Description
A Himalayan river contributing to the dynamic river system of the Dooars.  
It originates in the Bhutan hills and flows through the plains, playing a role in shaping the region’s hydrology and landscape.

---

## Course & Flow
1. Originates in the Bhutan hills  
2. Flows southward into the Dooars  
3. Continues across the plains toward Bangladesh  

---

## Importance
- Contributes to the overall river network of the Dooars  
- Influences flooding patterns  
- Supports agriculture and local livelihoods  
- Shapes settlement distribution  

---

## Physical Features
- Carries debris and sediments from hills  
- Seasonal variation in flow  
- Prone to flooding during monsoon
