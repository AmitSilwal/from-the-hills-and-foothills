# Jaldhaka River

## 🟢 Quick Facts
- **Type:** Himalayan river  
- **Region:** Dooars  
- **Flow Direction:** North (Bhutan hills) → South (plains)  
- **Role in Dooars:** Important river influencing irrigation and geography  

---

## Description
A Himalayan river flowing through the Dooars region.  
It originates in the Bhutan hills and flows into the plains, contributing to the hydrology and landscape of the region.

---

## Course & Flow
1. Originates in the Bhutan hills  
2. Flows southward into the Dooars  
3. Continues across the plains toward Bangladesh  

---

## Importance
- Important for irrigation and agriculture  
- Influences local geography and settlement  
- Contributes to the river system of the Dooars  
- Supports local livelihoods  

---

## Physical Features
- Seasonal fluctuations in water flow  
- Carries sediments from hills  
- Prone to flooding during monsoon
