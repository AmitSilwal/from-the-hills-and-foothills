Falakata

Falakata is a town located in the Alipurduar district of West Bengal in the eastern Dooars.

Historical Connection

The name Falakata is believed to be derived from the historical estate Ambaree Fallacottah, mentioned in nineteenth-century frontier records.

This estate became a subject of dispute between Bhutan and British authorities and was placed under British management in 1842.
