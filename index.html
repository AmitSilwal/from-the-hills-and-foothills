From the Hills and Foothills

This digital archive explores the history of the Dooars region and the political developments that shaped the Eastern Himalayan frontier. It focuses on key events such as the Ashley Eden Mission (1864), the Dooar War, and the Treaty of Sinchula (1865).

The archive presents structured timelines, historical notes, and geographical insights to help understand how the Duars region transitioned from Bhutanese control to British administration.
