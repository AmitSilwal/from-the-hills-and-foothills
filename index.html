From the Hills and Foothills

A historical archive of the Dooars region — documenting frontier systems, wars, places, and societies.

About the Project

This archive is a structured research project focused on the history of the Dooars, including the Bhutan frontier system, the Duar War, and the transformation of the region under British rule.

Project Methodology

The project uses primary historical sources, structured note-taking, and interconnected research nodes to build a navigable knowledge system.
