# Precolonial Settlement of the Dooars

Before the nineteenth century, the Dooars region was sparsely populated and largely covered by forests.

Settlement was mainly concentrated near rivers and small cultivation patches.

## Main Communities

The primary inhabitants included:

- Mechis
- Lepchas
- other small indigenous groups

These communities practiced:

- shifting cultivation
- forest-based subsistence
- small-scale agriculture.
