# Rungeet River

The Rungeet River is a tributary of the Teesta River flowing through the eastern Himalayan region.

It forms part of the river systems connecting the Darjeeling hills and the Dooars plains.

## Geographic Context

The river is important for understanding the geography of the eastern Himalayan frontier.
