# Buxa

Buxa is a strategic mountain pass located in the eastern Dooars near the Bhutan frontier.

Historically the pass connected the plains of the Dooars with routes leading into Bhutan.

## Historical Mentions

### 1864 Duar War

During the [[Duar_War_1864_1865.md]], British forces advancing from [[Cooch_Behar]] captured the pass at [[Buxa]].

Control of Buxa allowed British forces to secure an important gateway between the plains and the Bhutan hills.

## Geographic Context

The area surrounding Buxa later became known for:

- Buxa Fort
- frontier trade routes
- strategic military movement between Bhutan and the plains.
