# Koomrekatta

Koomrekatta served as a British operational camp during the later phase of the [[Duar_War_1864_1865.md]].

After Bhutanese counterattacks near [[Dewangiri]], British forces regrouped at Koomrekatta before preparing further operations in the frontier region.
