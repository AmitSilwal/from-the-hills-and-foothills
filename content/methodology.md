# Research Methodology

## About the Project

From the Hills and Foothills is an independent research and documentation initiative focused on the historical landscape of the Eastern Himalayan foothills, particularly the Dooars region and adjoining hill areas.

## Research Approach

The archive is built through systematic reading of historical sources and extraction of relevant information.

Focus areas include:

- historical events  
- geographical locations  
- administrative developments  
- frontier interactions  
- settlements and communities  

Each reference is recorded with attention to its original source.

## Sources

The project relies on:

- district gazetteers  
- colonial administrative reports  
- settlement records  
- travel narratives  
- historical publications  
- archival documents  

## Organization

Research notes are structured into:

- historical events  
- geographical locations  
- timelines  
- thematic notes  
- source references  

## Project Status

This is an ongoing research project. Content is added progressively.
