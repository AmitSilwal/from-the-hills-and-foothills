# Archive Rules

## Purpose

This archive documents and preserves Nepali (Gorkhali) culture respectfully.

## Content Allowed

- Public places and landscapes  
- Everyday life and work  
- Cultural events  
- Language and traditions  
- Historical materials  

## Content Not Allowed

- Private individuals  
- Sensitive moments  
- Political messaging  
- Misleading content  

## Credit & Sourcing

Every item must include:

- Creator  
- Source  
- Platform  
- Link  

## Tone

- Neutral  
- Respectful  
- Observational  
- Archival  

## Corrections

All correction or removal requests will be handled respectfully.
