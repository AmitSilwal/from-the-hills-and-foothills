About the Project

From the Hills and Foothills is an independent research and documentation project focused on the historical landscape of the Eastern Himalayan foothills and the Dooars region.

The project seeks to collect, organize, and document historical references related to the region, including frontier history, settlement patterns, and geographical locations mentioned in historical sources.

Much of the historical information about the Dooars region remains scattered across colonial records, gazetteers, and historical publications. This archive aims to gradually bring together these references into a structured and accessible digital resource.

The project is currently in an early research phase. Materials and findings will be added progressively as research continues.
