Place Index of the Dooars

This page lists geographical locations mentioned in historical sources related to the Dooars region and the Eastern Himalayan foothills. The index will expand as research continues.

Place Type Notes
Buxa Duar / frontier pass Important Bhutan frontier gateway in the Dooars.
Chechakhata Settlement Frontier settlement associated with early British administration.
Dalimkot Fort area Strategic hill location connected with the frontier region.
Bijni Region / estate Area involved in late eighteenth century frontier disputes.
