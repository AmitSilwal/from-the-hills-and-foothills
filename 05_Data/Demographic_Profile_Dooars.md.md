# Demographic Profile – Dooars Region

## Administrative Context
- Major districts: Alipurduar, Jalpaiguri.
- Census-based data varies by district.

## Population Overview (Census 2011)

### Jalpaiguri District
- Total population: 3,869,675
- Literacy rate: 73.79%
- Sex ratio: 954
- Predominantly rural population

### Alipurduar Region (pre-2014 district formation)
- Approx. population: ~1.7 million (subdivision basis)
- Literacy rate: ~78–79%
- Highly rural and plantation-dependent

## Structural Characteristics
- Rural-dominated demographic profile.
- Significant tea plantation labour concentration.
- Mixed ethnic composition (Adivasi, Rajbanshi, Nepali-speaking communities).
- Economic dependence on tea + agriculture.
